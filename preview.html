01/ Identity Manifesto

Restraint is the primary move.

Terminal gravity meets editorial restraint. Dark by default, near-black rather than charcoal. Monospace carries all data. The palette is monochrome first. Green carries positive emphasis and completion; red is reserved for attention, anomaly, or urgent live-now state. Cards float on contrast, not shadow. Every element justifies its presence.

10/ Data Motif — Two New Siblings Same primitive · one quantitative · one qualitative
A
DotTimeline
Quantitative. 24 time buckets over 60s, each a vertical stack of up to 10 dots. Dot count = event density. Column color = bucket state. Hover a column to pulse it; direct sibling of DottedChart with a time axis.
60s agonow
B
PulseTrail
Qualitative — Emil-style signature motion. A larger accent-live head dot (8px, with a soft halo) sweeps left→right on a 4s loop while pulsing on the LiveDot cadence; smaller trail dots mark prior events at decaying opacity, and pulse on their own when hovered. Motion IS the data.
60s agonow
11/ Do & Don't Enforced by the Quality Evaluator

DO

  • Use weight and luminance for hierarchy, not color
  • Set data in mono with tabular-nums
  • Reach for 1px border before shadow
  • Keep motion under 300ms
  • Start entry from scale(0.95), never scale(0)
  • Make exits ~20% faster than entries
  • Set transform-origin explicitly on popovers
  • Use … for truncation, not ...
  • Cap body at 65ch
  • Show loading with mono status text or segmented progress when total is known

DON'T

  • Write transition: all
  • Use a shadow with blur ≥ 25px (the AI-slop shadow)
  • Transition color on hover (opacity instead)
  • Use Inter, Roboto, system-ui as the primary face
  • Color a CTA button with accent-live or accent-success
  • Scatter green and red through one cluster without a clear positive/attention mapping
  • Apply gradient fills to buttons, cards, or backgrounds
  • Use radius above lg on a dark card
  • Stretch body text full viewport width
  • Use layout-mimic placeholder chrome for loading instead of explicit status
